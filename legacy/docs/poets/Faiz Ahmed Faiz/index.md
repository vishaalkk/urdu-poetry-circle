# Faiz Ahmed Faiz

<div style="text-align: center; margin: 4rem 0;">
  <p style="font-size: 1.4rem; font-style: italic; max-width: 700px; margin: 0 auto;">
    Faiz Ahmed Faiz (1911–1984) was a Pakistani poet and author of Urdu literature. One of the most celebrated poets of the Urdu language, Faiz was nominated for the Nobel Prize in Literature and won the Lenin Peace Prize.
